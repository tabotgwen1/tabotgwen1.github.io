About The Ambazonian Voice

The Ambazonian Voice is a movement dedicated to amplifying the voices of the Ambazonian people, promoting unity, justice, and freedom for all. Our goal is to engage communities, educate citizens, and work toward a future where every voice is heard.

Our Values

  • Unity: Bringing people together for a common cause.
  • Justice: Advocating fairness and equal rights.
  • Freedom: Empowering people to determine their own future.
  • Transparency: Open and honest leadership at every level.

Ambazonian Community In Cyprus

Those in Cyprus have decided to come together to make their voices heard by internationalizing the struggle so that a lasting solution can be found. Also to help assist displaced ones back home and support other refugees in countries like Ghana and Nigeria. We also help our members in time of problems since we are far from our families.

What We Do

We organize educational programs, community outreach initiatives, and advocacy campaigns. We aim to inform the public about the history, rights, and aspirations of Ambazonia, creating a platform for dialogue and action.
